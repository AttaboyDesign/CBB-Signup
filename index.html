Book Drive

Date: June 5, 2021
Time: 11:00AM - 5:00PM

Volunteers are the heart and soul of the Children's Book Bank!

Join us at our Annual Book Drive! Our book drive supplies children's books to hundreds of families in need. Help us by participating in our event while we help collect, deliver and host events for our community that can help change a child's life.

June 5th, 2021

Organization/s: Children's Book Bank
Location: Portland, OR
Time: Set up at 9am-clean up at 5:30pm
Activity type: Collection, Sorting, Set up, other

Responsibilities:

Expect to collect, distribute and sort donated books from our book drive event. We will also need help setting up and taking down our event materials.

Age: 14+ or accompanied by a guardian
Reserve: You must reserve slots for you and any accompanied members for the event at least 24 hours in advance to the event.
Waiver: If not 18 years and older, you must have a waiver signed by a parent or guardian.

Our process at Children's Book Bank is to sign up for our event below, you will receive a confirmation email with more details about the event. If we need any further information we will reach out directly via email.

Dress for the weather, wear long pants, wear sturdy, closed-toe shoes and bring a water bottle. Face coverings will be required at our events. Please bring your own face covering, gloves, water and snacks. PP&R will provide face coverings, tools and gloves when needed.
